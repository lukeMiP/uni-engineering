Biped Walking Robot

A Walking Robot trained using reinforcement learning. STILL IN PROGRESS.

Motivation.

This project began as an opportunity for me to deepen my knowledge and understanding of using AI and Machine Learning to train Physical Robots to complete tasks, primarily walking.

Progress.

I am currently in the early stages of design and research for this project. The majority of the body will be constructed using aluminium extrusion and 3D printed joiners. The joints will be driven using 12V DC Motors with encoders connected through a custom designed and a 3D printed Cycloidal Gearbox. See Current CAD Progress Below.
